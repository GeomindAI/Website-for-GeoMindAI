About GeoMind AI

GeoMind AI builds software for exploration geologists. Founded by a practicing P.Geo with direct field experience, we develop tools that address real pain points in mineral exploration — from predictive prospectivity mapping to offline-capable field data collection.

Mineral Prospectivity Mapping

Hadoona
