How secure is my data?

We provide on-premise analysis or secure cloud processing, depending on your needs.

What output formats are supported?

TIFF, GeoJSON, and Point Samples—fully compatible with GIS platforms.

Can I audit the model?

Yes, we welcome audits and can work with your team to ensure transparency.

What resolution options are available?

Resolutions from 5m to 1km are customizable based on your requirements.
